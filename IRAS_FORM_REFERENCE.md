## Why this matters for the app

The form already collects every **simple structured field** directly (radios,
multi-selects, Yes/No). The app should **not** re-collect or "map" those as
paste-in text — it should just help the user **verify they have that information**.

The app's real job is the **two free-text fields** (marked **★ FREE TEXT** below),
which are the hard, open-ended ones a person struggles to word well:

1. **"Provide as much detail as possible about the tax evasion or tax fraud."**
2. **"Explain how and when you became aware of the tax evasion or tax fraud."**

---

## Instructions (shown at the top of the form)

- Completing the form accurately helps keep the tax system fair.
- The form must be **completed in one session** — it **cannot be saved** for
  later editing. (The form shows an estimated completion time; confirm the exact
  figure on the live form.)
- You can **upload supporting attachments** (a limited number of files, with a
  maximum size per file — confirm the current limits on the live form).
- IRAS keeps your identity and the information provided **confidential**. You may
  also remain **anonymous**.
- Due to tax secrecy provisions, IRAS **will not update you** on the status of the
  case or disclose any follow-up action.
- There are **penalties for giving false or misleading information** to a public servant.

## Section: Your contact details

IRAS keeps your identity as an informant confidential. You may also choose to
remain anonymous. If you provide contact details, IRAS may contact you for
clarification or additional information to help investigations.

*All fields are mandatory unless stated otherwise.*

### Cash rewards for informants
A reward based on **15% of the tax recovered, capped at S$100,000**, may be given
to informants if the information and/or documents provided lead to a recovery of
tax that would otherwise have been lost. To be considered for a reward:

- You must provide your **full name, identity number, and contact details** in this
  report. IRAS may request you to come in for identity verification. Informant
  identities are kept strictly confidential.
- You must provide **relevant and specific information** with supporting documents
  or evidence showing the tax evasion or fraud.
- You **must not** have participated in, assisted, abetted, or "planned and
  initiated" the tax evasion or fraud.
- You **must not** have provided false or misleading information to IRAS.
- You must render **full cooperation** to IRAS, including testifying in court as a
  witness (not as an informer) if requested.

All payments are made **solely at the discretion of the Commissioner of IRAS**,
after the investigation is completed and taxes are collected. IRAS will contact
informants who are to be rewarded and will not provide status updates.
